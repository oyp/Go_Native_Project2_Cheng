Top 10 Beautiful Cities

Sydney, Australia

The Sydney Opera House is a world heritage site and hosts more than 1500 performances a year.

New York, United States

New York is home to some of the best museums in the world including Metropolitan museum of arts, American museum of natural history, Frick collection and Guggenheim museum.

Singapore City, Singapore

The infinity pool in the 57th storey of Marina Bay Sands of Singapore is the largest infinity pool in the world.

Cape Town, South Africa

Mixed colored houses in Bo-kaap,Central Cape Town have Georgian and Dutch architectural styles.

Rio De Janeiro, Brazil

Christ the Redeemer, situated on 700 meters high Corcovado mountain is the most famous attraction of Rio de Janeiro and an icon of Christianity.

Istanbul, Turkey

Hagia Sophia is a former Byzantine church and now a museum. It was built back in the 4th century. Topkapi Palace, Sultan Ahmet imperial mosque, Galata tower and Ephesus are some other famous architectural marvels of Istanbul.
